PR!SM Data Management

PR!SM is a Medical Provider Data Management application that is cloud based and in use currently by clients of Intellegenex, the creator of the software. PR!SM allows a full range of data manipulations including updating the 30+ Provider Search sites used to find medical providers for Workers Comp related health care.
